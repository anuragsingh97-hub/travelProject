About Lloyd

Lloyd Group of Institutions is located in modern township, Greater Noida of Delhi NCR. The premier group offers programs in Engineering, Technology, Law, Management, Pharmacy and Education across 20+ lush green acres.

With more than 22 years of excellence as an educational provider, Lloyd has marked quality across all disciplines through skill-based education.

Lloyd offers B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA approved by A.I.C.T.E and affiliated to AKTU University.
